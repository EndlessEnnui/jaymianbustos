Jojo's Bizarre Adventure: What if Scenario



A what-if scenario from the Jojo's Bizarre Adventure: Stardust Crusaders anime series in which the previous protagonist of the series, Joseph Joestar, reverts to his younger self to face his generational family nemesis, Dio Brando. Everything presented in this video was made in Adobe Illustrator and then animated through Adobe AfterEffects.
