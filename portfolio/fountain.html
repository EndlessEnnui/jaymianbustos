Drowned in Oil

Previous Next

A series of fountains conceptualized in photoshop to raise awareness of oil spills and their detrimental effects on marine life and their ecosystems. The main objective of these fountain designs are to capture the public's attention using their oil slick texture and witness the horror and pain that oil companies and oil consumers have inflicted upon these animals. For this public art proposal, I would place these fountains along popular beach cities around the United States and potentially in front of big oil companies to remind the of the damages they have caused due their greed.
