Digital Enviroment









As technology advances throughout the decade, the more realistic virtual reality becomes and the harder it is to differentiate the virtual world from the physical world. To demonstrate such a fact, I took photographs inside of Red Dead Redemption 2â€™s hyper-realistic virtual world and glitched them in order to make it so that differentiating the photos from virtual and physical would be nearly impossible and leaving some of the original photos for the viewers to decide themselves. For each photo, I have converted them into made several alternate versions of them through Audacity and edited and composited them through photoshop to take the best elements of each variation and compile them into a single photo. I made the effort to try and leave the main subject of each photo unedited to let the audience know that although the original images may seem real, they are merely photographs taken inside the virtual world.
