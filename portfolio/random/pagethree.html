Artist Statement

Presented here is my Original Character Slot Machine. I wanted to create something similar to a video game that describes characters that I and my significant other have made in the past. During the creation of this project, I began teaching myself pixel art on Aseprite. So I used this project as a means to practice creating assets for games I would want to make in the future.

Features:
Button that randomizes 5 different icons corresponding to a character/item.

Upon getting three of the same icon, text will appear describing the icon and victory music will play.

Jackpot (Character) shifts side to side.

Music plays/pauses by pressing 1 and 2 respectively.
