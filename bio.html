Hello, my name is Jaymian Bustos. I am an aspiring artist studying Digital Media Art at San Jose State Univeristy. Ever since I got my hands on my first ever game (Pokemon Pearl for the Nintendo DS) I have an ever growing facsination with digital art and coding. I'm the type of person who wants to do it all, whether it be coding, photography, design, video, storytelling etc. and once I get hooked on a certain thing I will try my best to learn every single aspect about it. The more skills the better, hence why I chose Digital Media Art as the major covers such a large variety of areas in the digital world. Recently I have been delving into the realm of pixel art and coding in order to make my own assets for games and other such projects I decide to pursue in the future. Some of my preffered programs/methods to make art are Photoshop, Illustrator, Java/JavaScript, Aesprite, and AfterEffects with Unity being one of the programs I really want to dive into to start making games myself. I hope to one day gain enough experience to make something that people can look forward to at the end of the day whether is be a video game, a web series, or something entirely new. I want to give the same feeling of excitement or joy from the media I consumed to the next generation and hopefully inspire them to do the same one day.
